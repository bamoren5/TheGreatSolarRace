The Elves of Venus

The elven race belongs to the planet Venus, the hottest planet in the solar system. Unlike the dwarves from Mercury, the elves embrace the heat in their culture and believe it is a part of them spiritually. Being the second planet also makes them wealthy but not as much as Mercury, it’s not even close. Imagine that in total the dwarves had a million dollars, the elves in comparison would only have hundred thousand. Considering the heat stays trapped in Venus, much of benefits given from the sun don’t outweigh the amount of heat they get and have to deal with. This leads the elves to be extremely envious of the dwarves and now they have a sort of a rivalry.
